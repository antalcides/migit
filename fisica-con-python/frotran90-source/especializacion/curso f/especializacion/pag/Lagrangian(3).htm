Why do they apply?
The problem asks for the definition of a cyclic variable and its relationship to conservation laws.
How do they apply?
(a)  The Lagrangian is a function of the generalized coordinates and velocities and time.  Any generalized coordinate, which does not appear explicitly in the Lagrangian, is said to be cyclic.
